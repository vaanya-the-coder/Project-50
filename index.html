Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas are great at camouflaging in their environment.
  • Pandas are very lazy animals - eating and sleeping make their day.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • They can recognise each letter of the human alphabet and differentiate between photographs.
  • Pigeons are very good at multitasking.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt.
  • A teddy has been in space, taken by a man who went.
  • September 9th is celebrated as National Teddy Bear Day!
